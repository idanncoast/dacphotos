Who Am I?

I am Daniel Costa (DAC)

I’m a 29-year-old photographer and designer with a passion for capturing the world as I see it. I love going out with my camera—exploring the streets, nature, architecture—and turning everyday moments into visual stories.

This is important Do what you love, and do it again

For me, photography is about more than just taking pictures. It’s about feeling something, seeing beauty in the ordinary, and expressing what words can’t always say. Whether it’s the quiet of a forest or the lines of a building, I try to freeze moments that speak for themselves.

How it all started...

It all began when I was 16. My parents gave me a camera, and from the moment I held it, something clicked—literally and creatively. I started taking photos of everything around me, just exploring the world through a new lens. What began as curiosity quickly turned into a passion.

Design

Over time, I realized photography wasn't just a hobby it was a way for me to express how I see things, how I feel moments. As I grew, I also got into web design. I loved building things visually, shaping ideas into something people could interact with.
So I decided to bring those two passions together: photography and web design. Now, I not only capture images—I create the spaces where they live. My work is a mix of what I see and how I choose to share it with the world.

Future plans

Hobbies and Free Time Activities

My Camera

I shoot with a Canon EOS 200D lightweight yet powerful camera that’s been with me through a lot of creative moments. It’s compact enough to carry anywhere, but still gives me the control and quality I need to capture the kind of photos I imagine.

Playing

I’m a big fan of video games—they’re a great way to relax, explore new worlds, and challenge myself. Whether it’s story-driven adventures or fast-paced action, gaming is one of my favorite ways to unwind and get inspired.

Coding

I love building websites using HTML and CSS because it lets me create the perfect space to showcase my photography. Designing and coding my own site gives me the freedom to bring my vision to life and share my work exactly how I imagine it.

Wierd stuff

I’ve always been fascinated by animals—especially bugs and fishes. Their little worlds are full of surprises and beauty, and I love learning about them and capturing their unique charm whenever I can. Nature’s diversity never stops amazing me.
