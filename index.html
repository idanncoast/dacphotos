About me

I'm 29 years old and photography is more than just a hobby to me — its how I connect with the world.

I love going out, walking through streets, nature, or hidden corners of the city, and capturing what catches my eye. For me, its all about the feeling in a moment—the light, the colors, the raw details that tell a story.

Every photo I take begins with something simple: a curiosity, a dream, a mood. I dont stage life—I chase it, frame by frame. Whether it's a quiet street at sunset or a burst of energy in a crowded place, I try to turn what I see into something that others can feel.

Projects I am currently working on

Nature Through My Eyes

This project is a personal journey into the quiet beauty of nature. Through my lens, I try to capture the details that often go unnoticed—the way light filters through leaves, the textures of earth and stone, the stillness of water, or the movement of wind in grass.
It's not just about landscapes it's about the emotion in natural moments.

Lines and Stories

This project is my way of exploring the beauty of architecture—not just in buildings, but in the shapes, lines, textures, and rhythms that define the spaces around us. I’m drawn to the balance between structure and light, symmetry and imperfection, old and new. Through my lens, I capture the character of cities, the quiet details of design, and the way architecture interacts with its environment. Each photo is a moment where structure becomes art—and where space tells its own story.
